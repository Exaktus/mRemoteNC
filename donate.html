Donate

Thank you for coming here!

Currently, money donations is not needed. But your help would be very useful. You can help in several ways:
  • Help translating mRemoteNC to your language
    • It is easy and does not take a lot of time.

  • Report a bug or make a feature request
    • Please inform us, if you found bug in mRemoteNC or in site. Or about the features that you are missing.
    We do not have enough testers.

  • Join as a programmer
    • mRemoteNC is open source software. You can join development, fix bugs, implement new features.
    Please, contact us, if you are interested in. Or make direct pull request.

  • Join as a technical writer
    • If you have a good language, you can write documentation, FAQs or manuals.

  • Help mRemoteNC become more beautiful
    • If you are a graphic designer, contact us.

  • Host mRemoteNC setup files
    • We need a place, where keep and share mRemoteNC releases.

  • Tell your friends about us :)
    • Write about mRemoteNC in your blog, post link on your site, share link on social sites.
